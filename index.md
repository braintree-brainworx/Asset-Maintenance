# Asset Maintenance
Braintree's Fixed Assets Maintenance extension provides additional facilities for managing your fixed assets. Features include:

**Fixed asset transfers**
Move assets between physical locations or dimensions, using the FA transfer document, with Ship and Receive functions, and a detailed audit trail.

**Fixed asset usage**
Track utilisation of fixed assets based on hours, kilometres or other units of measure

**Maintenance plans**
Create multiple maintenance plans for each asset maintenance group.

**Maintenance orders**
Generate maintenance instructions for fixed assets, based on maintenance plans.

**Procurement integration**
Generate purchase orders for maintenance activities.
